PitchSmart

Empowering youth baseball players and coaches by using data to prevent injuries and optimize performance.

Why PitchSmart?

Youth baseball pitchers are vulnerable to overuse injuries. While pitch count guidelines exist, many decisions around rest and recovery are made based on current success, not protecting arm health. This can lead to arm fatigue, injury, or long-term damage.

PitchSmart is a tool built by Sid Suri after he underwent arm injuries to help coaches and players track pitch counts, monitor recovery, and make better decisions on frequency of pitching.
