3-Day Recovery Band Routine

Day 1

Exercises to do:

  • External rotations – 2 sets of 15 reps
  • Internal rotations – 2 sets of 15 reps
  • Scapular retractions – 2 sets of 12 reps
  • Face pulls – 2 sets of 12 reps

Day 2

Exercises to do:

  • External rotations – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Internal rotations – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Face pulls – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Scapular retractions – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lateral raises with band – 2 sets of 12 reps

Day 3

Exercises to do:

  • External rotations – 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Internal rotations – 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Face pulls – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Scapular retractions – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Lateral raises with band – 2 sets of 15 reps
  • Arm circles – 2 sets of 20 seconds each direction
